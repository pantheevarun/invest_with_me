Our Story

Welcome to Invest-With-Me, a dedicated platform founded by Barun Panthi with a singular mission: to democratize share market knowledge and make NEPSE (Nepal Stock Exchange) information accessible to every Nepali investor.

Established in 2020, Invest-With-Me has grown from a small blog to one of Nepal's most trusted sources for stock market analysis, NEPSE updates, and investment education. We understand that the share market can be intimidating, especially for beginners. That's why we break down complex financial concepts into simple, actionable insights.

Our platform serves thousands of readers monthly, providing them with:

  • Daily NEPSE market updates and analysis
  • In-depth technical and fundamental analysis
  • IPO news, reviews, and recommendations
  • Investment strategies for beginners and experts
  • Educational content on share market basics

About the Founder - Barun Panthi

Barun Panthi

Founder & Chief Analyst

Barun Panthi is a passionate stock market analyst and financial educator based in Nepal. With years of hands-on experience in the secondary share market and deep understanding of NEPSE operations, Barun has helped thousands of investors make informed decisions.

His journey in the stock market began with personal investments, which quickly evolved into a mission to educate others. Barun believes that financial literacy is the key to wealth creation, and through Invest-With-Me, he shares his knowledge, research, and market insights freely with the community.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and actionable share market information that empowers every Nepali investor to make informed investment decisions. We strive to bridge the gap between complex financial markets and everyday investors through simplified analysis and education.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's most trusted and comprehensive online resource for NEPSE and share market information. We envision a financially literate Nepal where every citizen has access to quality investment knowledge and can participate confidently in the capital market.
